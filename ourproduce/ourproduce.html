lamb

The lamb at Drivers Farms is 100% free-range and pasture fed. We take care to ensure that the lambs have sufficient food, water and shelter at all times to give them the best quality of life possible and to give you, the customer, the piece of mind that our lambs are treated with the best of care.

beef

The cattle herd graze our fields openly for 8 months of the year, and are only housed when the weather becomes too harsh for them to stay outdoors. Therefore, our beef is not 100% pasture fed, but we do feed them rolled wheat and barley that has been grown by us, which provides a lovely marbled meat.
