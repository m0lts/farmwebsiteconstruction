Our sheep flock have been on the farm since 2008, grazing on the permanent pastures dotted around Sawston. The lambs raised on these pastures are therefore 100% pasture-fed.

Ever since Grandad John decided to change from milking to a beef cows, the farm has been home to a beautiful herd of Limousin cattle who graze on the meadowland at the rear of the farmyard.

Only recently, Richard made the decision to implement regenerative farming practices which aim to integrate, rehabilitate and protect natural processes in our farming system.
