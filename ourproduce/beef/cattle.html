Cows

The cows at Drivers Farms are all Limousin cattle - the Limousin breed originates from France and is well known for its health, adaptability and low fat beef. We chose the Limousin breed because they are great at coping with the meadowlands on which we let them range. Additionally, the breed's natural traits of good health reduces the requirement of our intervention, especially with antibiotics, which is a growing issue in the livestock industry.

Beginning from January, the herd is housed indoors in one of our barns to shelter the young calves and rest of the herd from the harsh winter conditions. Approaching the start of May, the herd is then released out into the fields around the farmyard to range and graze freely for 8 months of the year, until Novemeber time, when they must be housed indoors again.
